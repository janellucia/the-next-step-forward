What to do with unwanted books?

TNSF TIP #1: What to do with unwanted books? I believe that books should be read, re-read and loved. it's a great idea to give them a new home. It seems these days that Libraries & Charities don't want them. My recommendation? Secondlifebooks.net

They provide free pickup of books; they donate books to youth centres, homeless shelters and correctional facilities across the GTA; and they resell books on Amazon. This is a fantastic service that gives books a new home, gives back to the community and provides an eco-friendly way of giving books a new home. I am a fan.

Author: Clare Platt, CEO, The Next Step Forward Inc.
